Cybersecurity Incidents

Learn about recent cybersecurity incidents, their effects, and how they were handled.

A cybersecurity incident is an event that poses a threat to the confidentiality, integrity, or availability of information systems or data.
It can be an unwanted or unexpected event that compromises business operations or has a significant risk of doing so.
Examples include phishing attacks, ransomware, insider threats, and data breaches.
